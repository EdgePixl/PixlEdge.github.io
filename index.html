About Us

Welcome to Pixl Edge!


At Pixl Edge, we are on the brink of an exciting new venture in the world of art and technology. With a strong foundation in technology, we are poised to delve into the realm of NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens). Our upcoming NFT project is driven by our passion for creativity and innovation.


With our newest project, JingerNFTs, we aim to leveragethe power of blockchain technology and digital art to redefine the way art is created, shared, and valued. Through our NFT project, we seek to provide a platform for artists to showcase their unique creations, while also offering collectors the opportunity to own exclusive digital artworks with provable authenticity.


At Pixl Edge, we believe in the transformative potential of NFTs to revolutionize the art world. Stay tuned as we unveil our innovative approach to combining art, technology, and blockchain in the realm of NFTs. JingerNFT's first NFT collection is coming on July 8th!


Welcome to the future of digital art. Welcome to Pixl Edge!
